Stable tag: trunk

TwittLink Twitter Client

== Description ==

TwittLink Twitter Client is a widget for your website. It's a great way to connect your Twitter community to your website, and it gives your visitors the possibility to use Twitter without leaving your website. Twittlink Twitter Client can transform visitors from simple readers into someone sharing and communicating on topics from your website.

== Installation ==

Upload the TwittLink plugin to your blog, Activate it and done!
